Projects

AgriSoft empowers 250 young individuals by training them as farmers, modifying their skills through AgriSoft's platform for enhanced agricultural productivity and sustainability

We incorporated 50 crores + tech inventory into farming, which is serving nearly 100 + acers of fertile and non fertile lands.

We've cultivated 100+ acres, employing instant soil testing and cross-state transportation to enhance profitability and efficiency.

Agrisoft is a platform designed to connect landowners who have arable land but lack the time or expertise to cultivate it with skilled agricultural workers who can manage and yield crops effectively. This collaborative approach ensures that land is utilized efficiently and sustainably, benefiting both parties
